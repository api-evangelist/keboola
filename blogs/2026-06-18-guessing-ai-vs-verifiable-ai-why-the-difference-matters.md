The article distinguishes between "guessing AI" (AI connected to ungoverned financial data that makes reasonable but potentially incorrect assumptions) and "verifiable AI" (AI connected to a governed data layer with defined metrics and audit trails). The key insight is that the foundation matters more than the model, making proper data governance essential before deploying AI in finance.
