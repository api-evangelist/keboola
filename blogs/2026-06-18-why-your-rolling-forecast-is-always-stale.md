Rolling forecasts become outdated because the underlying data pipeline relies on manual, time-consuming processes rather than automated data flows. Automating the connection between operational systems and financial planning tools lets teams update forecasts weekly instead of quarterly while freeing analysts from data collection work to focus on analysis.
